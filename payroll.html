Payroll

  • WisERP Payroll module is a quick and easy way to manage your payroll processing
  • You can have hourly salary, monthly salary and Time & Material employees and calculate payroll monthly.
  • It's easy customizable & fully integrated with the rest of the software.
  • Multiple contract feature permits you to add multiple contracts for a single employee.
  • Generate a batch of pay slips at once and have all payslips ready in one go.
  • Freedom to access data of any given data, different versions of one record & manage changes in contract/employees
  • Time & Attendance machine integration, WisERP Payroll takes the timeconsuming task of processing a payroll and turns it into a simple automated process.
