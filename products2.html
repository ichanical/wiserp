product

WisERP includes a number of features usually only found in much more expensive software. It has extensive inventory-related features such as multi warehousing, multilevel price breaks and price assignment, serial number tracking, reorder tracking, multiple costing methods (weighted average, standard), backorder tracking, and much more!

WisERP Accounting is capable of handling Multi Company, multi-currency with option to consolidate Profit & Loss and Balance Sheets. Transactional level audit trial is available for Critical data such as finance, payroll etc. with User Id, transaction date with time.

Microsoft Office Integration permits you to upload your data to Word, Excel or PDF easily. Effortlessly e-mail reports to Customers, Suppliers, Business Partners and Salespersons. View multiple windows at the same time (even from different modules).
